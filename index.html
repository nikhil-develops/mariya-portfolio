Smart Lister AI-Powered Listing at Scale

AI workflow · Product design · Threecolts

I led the end-to-end product design of an AI assistant that auto-generates titles, descriptions, and categories while enabling bulk background removal reducing listing time by ~40% across 10,000+ SKUs with measurably higher data accuracy.

Shopify–eBay Integration Compliance-Critical Redesign

Design systems · Compliance · Threecolts

The existing app was failing Build for Shopify standards blocking it from the Shopify marketplace entirely. I led a full UX audit and redesign, migrated to Polaris, rebuilt core modules (Onboarding, Dashboard, Templates, Pricing), and closed 30+ UI/UX gaps. The result: BFS certification achieved and marketplace distribution unlocked.
